## What's Inside

### Trending Topics (2016-2025)
Multi-line chart tracking 8 major topics over time. Watch COVID dominate 2020, crypto spike in 2021, and AI/ML explode from 2023 onward.

### Sentiment Analysis
- Stacked sentiment bars by year (positive / neutral / negative)
- Topic-level sentiment breakdown showing which subjects spark joy vs outrage
- Polarity ranking of all 18 topics

### Topic Cloud
Word cloud sized by tweet volume and colored by sentiment. Sports and Taylor Swift glow green; Politics and Climate run red.

### Viral Tweets Table
Sortable table of sample viral tweets with engagement metrics and sentiment labels. Click headers to sort by likes, retweets, or year.

### User Segments
Five behavioral clusters identified through posting frequency, age, and engagement:
- Gen Z Creators (34% of tweets)
- Millennial Commentators (28%)
- Tech Enthusiasts (18%)
- Sports Fanatics (12%)
- Silent Scrollers (8%)

### Age Demographics
Tweet volume and sentiment broken down by age group. Gen Z posts the most but Millennials get more likes per tweet.

### Engagement Heatmap
Day-of-week × hour-of-day heatmap showing when Twitter lights up. Spoiler: Sunday nights are peak.

### Key Findings

- **COVID-19** had the most negative sentiment of any topic (-0.15 avg)
- **AI/ML** tweets grew 3.5x from 2023 and carry the most positive tech sentiment
- **Sports** tweets are the most emotionally volatile (widest sentiment swing)
- **NFT** mentions dropped 70% after 2022
- **Sunday 8-11 PM** has 3x more engagement than weekday mornings
